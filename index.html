About me

I have a PhD in Information and Communications Technology from Technical University of Madrid (UPM) in Spain since 2011. My research interests include software testing, web engineering, open source, multimedia and Java.

I work as a researcher at King Juan Carlos University (URJC) in Spain. We have created Kurento, an open source framework for the development of rich multimedia applications. We are now working on ElasTest, an elastic platform for end-to-end testing of large software systems.

I also work as an Assistant Professor at Centro Universitario de Tecnología y Arte Digital (U-tad) in Spain. I teach Introduction to Computer Networks and Web Programming.

I am the author of the book Mastering Software Testing with JUnit 5, edited by Packt Publishing.
