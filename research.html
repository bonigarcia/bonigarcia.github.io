Research Projects

I have partipated in different R&D projects (international, national, and regional) belonging to 5 different research groups:

  • 2017-2020: CodeURJC at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos (URJC).
  • 2013-2016: Systems and Communications Group (GSyC) at Universidad Rey Juan Carlos (URJC).
  • 2012-2013: Telematic System for Information and Knowledge Society (TSIC) at Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM).
  • 2006-2011: Real-Time Systems and Architecture of Telematic Services (STRAST) at Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM).
